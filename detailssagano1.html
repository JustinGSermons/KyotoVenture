Sagano Romantic Train

The Sagano Romantic Train is a charming sightseeing train that operates in the Saga-Arashiyama area of Kyoto, Japan.â€‹ It runs along the picturesque Hozu River, offering passengers stunning views of the surrounding natural landscapes, including lush forests and mountains. The train features nostalgic wooden cars and a fully open car, enhancing the relaxing experience of the scenic journey. The route spans approximately seven kilometers between Saga Torokko Station and Kameoka Torokko Station, taking about 25 minutes. Popular throughout the year, especially during the cherry blossom season in spring and the vibrant foliage in autumn, the Sagano Romantic Train provides a delightful escape into Japan's beautiful countryside and cultural heritage.

Location: Arashiyama District, Kyoto City
